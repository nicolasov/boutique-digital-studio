Designers of experiential conditions that outlive the moment.

blit. creates immersive visual environments for culture, brands, and moments meant to be shared.

since 2015, museums, cultural institutions, brands, and event creators have turned to blit. when the aim isn’t simply to show something, but to shape how it’s felt and how long it stays.

from digital scenography and large-scale installations to interactive and virtual experiences, blit. designs situations where space, story, and presence collide and meaning appears through experiencing, not watching.

no format is too big, no challenge too complex.

Alongside commissioned work, the studio authors blit. ORIGINALS, its own experiential platforms, not just one-off projects. Experiences like NOCTURNA™, ECHOES™, and PORTALIS™ are developed, owned, and evolved from within the studio, existing as ready-to-deploy platforms.

Some call it immersive.
Others call it experiential.
Audiences all say the same thing: It’s the thing that stays with you.

Technical Artist

We are looking for a talented and passionate Technical Artist to join our creative team. This role sits at the intersection of art and technology, acting as a key bridge between creative vision and technical execution. You will ensure that artistic concepts are efficiently and accurately implemented in real-time and offline engines, contributing to visually compelling, technically robust projects.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Bridge creative and technical teams across the production pipeline.
  • Design and optimize shaders, materials, and lighting for real-time and rendering engines.
  • Ensure visual quality, performance, and technical feasibility.
  • Define and improve tools, workflows, and technical standards.
  • Resolve complex technical and rendering challenges.

Core skills:

  • Expert knowledge of real-time rendering pipelines and PBR workflows.
  • Proven experience in VR and/or LBVR projects.
  • Advanced experience with shaders and materials in Unreal Engine and/or Unity.
  • Strong understanding of optimisation, lighting, and look development.
  • Proficiency with major DCC tools.
  • Senior-level problem solving and cross-disciplinary communication.

 

If you have a collaborative mindset, a strong set of design tools and a great team attitude, send us your CV+reel to jobs@blitstudio.com

Environment artist – Unreal engine

We are looking for a senior Environment Artist to join us on a specific project, responsible for creating high-quality 3D environments in Unreal Engine. This role is focused on cinematographic scenes, with strong attention to detail, lighting, composition, and atmosphere.
You will work closely with the creative and technical teams to build visually compelling environments designed for camera-driven shots rather than gameplay.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create detailed, high-quality 3D environments in Unreal Engine.
  • Develop cinematic scenes with strong composition, scale, and visual storytelling.
  • Define lighting, mood, and atmosphere to support cinematographic shots.
  • Integrate assets, materials, and set dressing with high attention to detail.
  • Optimize scenes while maintaining visual quality.
  • Collaborate with creative direction to ensure visual consistency.

Core skills:

  • Proven experience as an Environment Artist in Unreal Engine.
  • Strong sense of composition, lighting, and cinematic framing.
  • High attention to detail and visual polish.
  • Solid understanding of materials, shaders, and PBR workflows.
  • Experience with DCC tools such as Maya, Blender, or Cinema 4D.
  • Ability to work autonomously and take ownership of environment creation.

 

If you have a collaborative mindset, a strong set of design tools and a great team attitude, send us your CV+reel to jobs@blitstudio.com
